FAQ

Quick answers for guests planning a visit.

Everything you need to know about dining, ordering, and visiting Mykonos.

What kind of food does Mykonos serve?

Modern Greek Mediterranean dishes, fresh meza, authentic gyros, and shareable plates.

Where are you located?

71800 CA-111, Suite A122, Rancho Mirage at The River.

What are your hours?

Open daily from 10:30 AM to 10:00 PM.

Do you offer online ordering?

Yes, pickup and delivery are available through major platforms.

Can I make a reservation?

Yes, reservations are available online.
