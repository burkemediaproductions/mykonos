Family story

Greek roots, modern presentation, and hospitality that feels personal.

Ali Papis Jr. and wife Ebru, his dad Ali Sr. and mom Siyda bring together a family-led restaurant story shaped by more than two decades of cooking, hosting, and serving real Greek food with care.

The result is a destination designed for easy lunches, lively dinners, shareable plates, and that unmistakable feeling of being welcomed in.
